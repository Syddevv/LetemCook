# Let'em Cook

## Culinary Creations: Discover, Share, and Cook Together!

Let'em Cook is a vibrant online platform designed for passionate home cooks to share their culinary masterpieces, discover new recipes, and connect with a community of food enthusiasts. Whether you're looking to create your next kitchen experiment or find inspiration for dinner tonight, Let'em Cook has everything you need to cook and share!

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live_Demo-Visit%20Site-FF5722?style=for-the-badge&logo=google-chrome&logoColor=white)](https://letem-cook.vercel.app/)

## ‚ú® Features

Let'em Cook offers a rich set of features to enhance your cooking journey:

* **Discover Recipes:** Browse thousands of recipes from professional chefs and a growing community of home cooks. Find new flavors and expand your culinary horizons.
* **Share Creations:** Easily upload your own recipes with photos, detailed instructions, and ingredients. Share your unique dishes with the world!
* **Create, Edit, & Delete Recipes:** Full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality for your own recipes. Manage your culinary portfolio with ease.
* **Save Favorites:** Keep all the recipes you love in one convenient place by liking them.
* **Community Recipes:** Explore recipes shared by other users within the Let'em Cook community.
* **User Profile:** Each user has a personalized profile showcasing their cooking journey.
    * **Recipes Shared:** See how many recipes you've contributed to the community.
    * **Total Likes:** Track the total number of likes your recipes have received.
    * **Recipes Liked:** View the count of recipes you've favorited.
    * **Member Since:** See when you joined the Let'em Cook family.
    * **Most Liked Recipe:** Highlight your most popular recipe.
    * **Cooking Streak:** Stay motivated with a cooking streak tracker, showing how many consecutive days you've shared or liked recipes.
* **Intuitive Interface:** A clean and user-friendly design makes navigating the platform a breeze.
